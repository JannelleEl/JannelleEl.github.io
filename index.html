About Me

Hello, I'm Jannelle! A passionate and detail-driven 4th year BS Architecture student, constantly inspired by the harmony between form, function, and human experience.

My work blends creativity and technical precision, grounded in a deep appreciation for user-centered, sustainable, and contextual design. I’m always eager to learn and adapt, embracing the evolving trends of architecture to shape spaces that heal, inspire, and connect.
